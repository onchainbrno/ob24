> A week-long celebration of blockchain, freedom and privacy.

*Onchain Brno 2024* (#OB24) is a federation of events around cryptocurrencies happening at the end of October 2024 in [Brno, Czech Republic](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ABX0NLNcfJk).

Date: **October 21-27, 2024**

## WTF is Brno?

Brno = Cool city in Czech Republic. Lots of history, cool buildings, and stuff happening.
